# MISSoN-BP
cuffless blood pressure measurement using smartwatches
This is a part of cuffless blood pressure measurement Database for Myocardial Infarction & Stroke Screening & Intervention Of Nations (MISSoN) called MISSoN-BP
With the approval of the Institutional Review Board of Songshan Lake Central Hospital with No. DSY-IRB-2025-06, we recruited 5252 participants for a  single blood pressure experiment (S-BP) and 451 participants for follow-up pressure experiment (F-BP) lasting approximately four months.
